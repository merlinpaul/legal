Privacy Policy

Updated May 30, 2026

Trading AI's Privacy Policy describes how Trading AI collects, uses, and protects your personal data when you use our mobile application available on the Apple App Store. This policy applies globally and has been prepared to comply with the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), the California Consumer Privacy Act and California Privacy Rights Act (CCPA/CPRA), Brazil's Lei Geral de Protecao de Dados (LGPD), Canada's Personal Information Protection and Electronic Documents Act (PIPEDA), the UK GDPR, and other applicable international data protection frameworks. Where multiple frameworks apply, we follow the most protective standard.

What Is Personal Data at Trading AI?

We treat any data that relates to an identified or identifiable individual, or that is linked or linkable to them, as personal data, regardless of where that individual lives. This includes data that directly identifies you, such as your name, as well as data that does not directly identify you but that could reasonably be used to do so. Aggregated and fully anonymized data that cannot be connected to any individual is considered non-personal data for the purposes of this Privacy Policy.

Personal Data Trading AI Collects from You

We strive to collect only the personal data we need. The personal data we collect depends on how you interact with the App.

Chart images and analysis requests. When you use the AI analysis feature, you upload or capture financial market chart images. These images are transmitted to generate the analysis you requested. They are not stored permanently on our servers or any third-party server beyond the time strictly necessary to produce that analysis, and they are never used for any secondary purpose.

Support communications. If you contact us with a question, feedback, or support request, we may collect the content of your message and any contact information you choose to provide.

Technical and diagnostic data. The App may automatically collect limited technical information required for its operation, including device type and model, operating system version, application version, crash reports, performance diagnostics, and network connection type. This information does not directly identify you and is used solely to maintain the stability and performance of the App.

Anonymized analytics data. The App may use analytics tools to collect aggregated, anonymized data about how the App is used, such as features accessed, session duration, and performance metrics. This data cannot be used to identify individual users.

Data we do not collect. We do not collect your name, email address, financial account numbers, brokerage credentials, portfolio data, precise geolocation, device contacts, photos or media beyond images you explicitly submit for analysis, biometric data, health data, or advertising identifiers.

You are not required to provide data to us. However, if you choose not to submit chart images, the core analysis feature of the App cannot function.

Why Trading AI Uses Your Personal Data

We use personal data only for the purposes for which it was collected. These include delivering the AI chart analysis you requested, maintaining and improving the performance and security of the App, detecting and resolving technical issues, responding to support requests, preventing misuse or abuse of the service, and complying with applicable legal obligations. We do not use your data for advertising, behavioral profiling, or any purpose not described in this Privacy Policy. We do not engage in automated decision-making or profiling that produces legal or similarly significant effects on you.

Our Legal Basis for Using Your Data

For users in the European Economic Area (GDPR). We rely on performance of a contract (Article 6(1)(b)) to deliver the analysis you request; legitimate interests (Article 6(1)(f)) for crash reporting, diagnostics, and anonymized analytics, where our interests do not override your fundamental rights; your consent (Article 6(1)(a)) for sharing chart images with third-party AI systems, which you provide by actively submitting an image and may withdraw at any time; and legal obligation (Article 6(1)(c)) for disclosures required by law.

For users in California (CCPA/CPRA). We do not sell personal information. We do not share personal information for cross-context behavioral advertising. California residents have the right to know, delete, correct, and opt out of the sale or sharing of their personal information.

For users in other jurisdictions. For users in Brazil (LGPD), Canada (PIPEDA), the United Kingdom (UK GDPR), Australia (Privacy Act 1988), and other jurisdictions with applicable data protection law, we apply equivalent standards of transparency, consent, and data subject rights. The strictest applicable standard governs our practices in all cases.

AI Chart Processing and Third-Party AI Disclosure

Chart images you submit within the App are transmitted to third-party artificial intelligence systems to generate the analysis you requested. In accordance with Apple App Store Review Guideline 5.1.2(i), revised November 13, 2025, we expressly disclose that chart images and associated data you submit for analysis are shared with third-party AI providers for processing. By actively submitting an image for analysis, you provide explicit consent to this data transmission. You may withdraw this consent at any time by ceasing to use the analysis feature.

Images submitted for analysis are:

  • used exclusively to generate the output you requested
  • not retained on third-party AI servers beyond the real-time processing window
  • not used to train or fine-tune any AI model
  • not shared with any party other than the designated AI processing provider
  • transmitted exclusively over encrypted connections using TLS 1.2 or higher

The AI-generated analysis constitutes informational output only and does not constitute financial, investment, or trading advice of any kind.

Trading AI Does Not Share Your Personal Data

We do not sell, rent, license, or trade your personal information to any third party under any circumstances. Personal data may be disclosed only in the following strictly limited situations:

  • Service providers acting as data processors: third-party vendors, including AI processing systems and cloud infrastructure providers, who assist in operating the App and are bound by Data Processing Agreements consistent with GDPR Article 28 and equivalent international standards, prohibiting them from using your data for any other purpose.
  • Legal obligations: when required to comply with applicable law, regulation, court order, or lawful request from a competent public authority.
  • Protection of rights and safety: to protect the rights, property, or safety of the App, its users, or third parties, to the extent permitted by law.
  • Business transfer: in the event of a merger, acquisition, or asset sale, users will be notified before personal data is transferred and before it becomes subject to a materially different privacy policy.

How Trading AI Protects Your Personal Data

We implement industry-standard technical and organizational security measures to protect your information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, loss, or destruction. These include encrypted data transmission using TLS 1.2 or higher, strict access controls limiting data access to authorized personnel only, regular security assessments and vulnerability monitoring, and data minimization practices ensuring only necessary data is processed.

No method of internet transmission or electronic storage can be guaranteed completely secure. We cannot warrant absolute security of information transmitted through the App.

In the event of a personal data breach that is likely to result in risk to your rights and freedoms, we will notify the relevant supervisory authority within 72 hours of becoming aware of the breach, and will notify affected users without undue delay, as required by GDPR Articles 33 and 34 and equivalent provisions under applicable law.

How Long Trading AI Keeps Your Personal Data

We retain personal data only for as long as strictly necessary to fulfill the purposes described in this Privacy Policy and to meet our legal obligations:

  • Uploaded chart images: processed in real time and not retained beyond the analysis session.
  • Technical and diagnostic data: retained for a maximum of 90 days, after which it is securely deleted or fully anonymized.
  • Support communications: retained for a maximum of 24 months from the date of last contact, then permanently deleted.
  • Anonymized analytics data: retained in aggregated, non-identifiable form for product improvement purposes.

Upon expiration of the applicable retention period, all personal data is securely deleted or permanently anonymized such that re-identification is not possible.
